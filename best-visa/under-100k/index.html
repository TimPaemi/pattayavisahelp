Budget tier

Best Thailand visas under ฿100,000 / year

3 min read·Updated April 2026

Mid-tier visa budgets — when the LTR investment is too high but you want more security than a Marriage Non-O renewal cycle. Here are the realistic options between ฿20k and ฿100k/year.

The ฿20k-100k bracket

This is where realistic insurance, embassy paperwork, and agent assistance enter the picture. Includes the more "complete" retirement and family visas where the visa itself is cheap but ancillary costs (insurance, legalisation, agent fees) take you into mid-budget territory.

1. Retirement O-A — ฿40,000-90,000/year all-in

Issued from your home embassy. Up to 1-year stay. Mandatory THB 3M health insurance — premiums ฿40-80k/year for typical 50-65 year olds. Visa fee ~฿5,000 at embassy. Renewal at home embassy or in-country extension.

The insurance is the budget killer. For 70+ applicants, premiums can hit ฿120k+/year, pushing total over ฿150k. For 50-55s in good health, ฿40-50k/year is realistic.

2. Marriage Non-O with agent assistance — ฿20,000-40,000/year

Same Marriage Non-O as the under-฿20k tier, but with a Pattaya visa agent handling annual paperwork. Agent fees: ฿15-25k/year for the renewal package. Adds peace of mind for non-Thai-speakers — agent translates documents, queues at immigration, and pre-flags issues.

3. SMART Visa — ฿0 to ฿50,000 (case-by-case)

For tech experts, startup founders, executives, investors in S-curve industries. Visa fee waived in some categories. Free for accepted applicants from BOI-listed sectors. The cost lies in the application paperwork — endorsement letters, tech assessments, BOI submissions. Some applicants use lawyers (฿30-100k); some self-apply.

4. Education ED at premium school — ฿40,000-80,000/year

International language schools (Walen, Inlingua, AAA Thai), university-affiliated programs, or sports academies (Tiger Muay Thai, Sinbi Muay Thai). Tuition ฿40-80k/year for 4-12 hour/week programs. Visa fee ฿1,900.

5. DTV with agent + insurance ride-along — ฿30,000-60,000/year

DTV itself is ฿2k/year amortised. Layer in optional Pattaya health insurance (Cigna Global, BUPA Thailand, AIA — ฿20-40k/year), agent for 90-day reports (฿5-10k/year), and you are at ฿30-60k/year. Most DTV holders we advise pay this tier voluntarily for medical security.

What we recommend in this bracket

If you are 50+ and want embassy-issued security — Retirement O-A at ฿40-90k/year (insurance included).

If you are tech-credentialed — SMART Visa. Often free, requires effort but no fees.

If you have foreign income and want healthcare — DTV + Cigna Global at ฿30-60k/year.
