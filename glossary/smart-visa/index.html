SMART Visa — Skilled professional / startup visa

SMART Visa is the 4-year visa for tech experts (SMART-T), executives (SMART-E), investors (SMART-I), and startup founders (SMART-S) in BOI-promoted S-curve industries. Work permit waived. BOI endorsement required.
