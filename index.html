FAQ.

Are you an agent? Do you take commissions?

No. PVH is independent. If you want a specialist (lawyer or agent) we'll match you to a vetted Pattaya one — we don't take a cut from either side. You pay them direct.

Which visa is best for digital nomads in Pattaya?

DTV in 95% of cases. 5 years, 180 days per entry, remote-work permitted. Full breakdown at /visas/dtv/.

Do I have to pay Thai tax on foreign income?

Depends. LTR holders are exempt. Everyone else: foreign income remitted within the tax year of earning is taxable above 150K฿ if you stay 180+ days.

Can I trust the agents you recommend?

We've personally worked with them. We verify registration, check farang complaints, run quiet test cases. Anyone who slips, drops.

How does the contact process actually work?

Why email + WhatsApp, not phone calls?
