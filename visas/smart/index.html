What changed in February 2025

BOI Announcement Nos. 5/2568 and 6/2568 (issued 18 February 2025) eliminated the Talent (T), Investor (I), Executive (E), and Other/dependent (O) categories. Officials cited overlap with the LTR Visa as the reason. Only SMART-S — for entrepreneurs who have already established a certified startup in a targeted industry — remains active.

If you were issued a SMART-T/I/E/O before February 2025, your existing visa remains valid until expiry. Renewal will require switching tracks.

SMART-S benefits that survived

  • 2 years validity, multiple entry, renewable
  • Annual reporting only (vs 90-day for Non-B)
  • No re-entry permits needed
  • Work without a separate work permit in the endorsed startup
  • Fast-track service at OSOS (Bangkok) or EEC Labour Administration Center (Chonburi)

Pattaya / EEC angle

SMART-S holders running startups in EEC-promoted industries (Chonburi/Pattaya area: automotive, electronics, food, robotics, logistics) can use the EEC Labour Administration Center in Chonburi instead of Bangkok OSOS for visa issuance, extensions, and reporting. A meaningful time-saver for Pattaya-based founders.

SMART-S vs LTR — which is right now?

Post-reform the choice is much sharper. Both are BOI-managed and reduce reporting to annual.

  • SMART-S — only fits if you've already certified a startup in a targeted industry. 2-year validity. Faster path for founders.
  • LTR — broader: 4 sub-categories (Wealthy Global Citizen, Wealthy Pensioner, Work-from-Thailand Pro, Highly-Skilled Pro). 10-year validity. Tax exemption on foreign income for W/P/T; 17% flat rate for H.

Founder of a certified Thai startup? SMART-S is still simpler. Anyone else? LTR is now the route — that's where T/I/E applicants were redirected.

Deep dive

SMART Visa after the February 2025 reform: only S-track survives

The SMART Visa was Thailand's flagship long-stay program for high-skilled professionals, launched in 2018 with five tracks (T, I, E, S, O). The February 2025 reform consolidated this into a single track: SMART-S for highly-skilled professionals working in BOI-promoted sectors. The other tracks (Talent T, Investor I, Executive E, Other O) were discontinued or merged into LTR.

SMART-S: who actually qualifies (2026 rules)

  • Working in a BOI-promoted target industry (tech, biotech, automotive, aerospace, robotics, AI, EV manufacturing, medical, etc.)
  • Salary ≥ ฿100,000/month
  • Recognized expertise — typically degree + 5+ years experience, OR demonstrable industry contribution
  • Endorsement letter from your Thai employer (BOI-promoted) or relevant government agency
  • Health insurance (no specific minimum but recommended)

What SMART-S gives you that Non-B doesn't

  • 3 or 4-year visa (vs Non-B's annual extensions)
  • No work permit needed — the SMART Visa itself authorizes work
  • Annual report to immigration replaces 90-day reporting
  • Spouse and children get dependent visas with permission to work
  • Multiple-entry built in

Why most foreign professionals don't use SMART

  • Restricted to BOI-promoted target industries — most jobs don't qualify
  • Application process is more demanding than Non-B
  • LTR Highly-Skilled Professional often offers better terms (10 years vs 4) for similar profile
  • Thai employer often prefers Non-B + work permit because they're familiar with that paperwork

For most highly-skilled professionals, LTR is the better visa. SMART-S is primarily useful for those who don't quite hit LTR thresholds but still work in BOI-promoted sectors.

Application path for SMART-S

  1. Confirm your employer is BOI-promoted (check BOI promotion certificate)
  2. Get endorsement letter from BOI or relevant agency confirming your role qualifies
  3. Apply at SMART Visa One Stop Service Center in Bangkok (or via Thai embassy abroad)
  4. Provide salary documentation, employment contract, qualifications, BOI letter
  5. Approval typically 30 days; visa issued for 3 or 4 years matching contract

What changed in February 2025

The reform stripped SMART back to S-track only. T, I, E, O tracks were either discontinued or absorbed into the LTR program. This was partly because most applicants for those tracks were already routing to LTR after its 2022 launch, and partly to simplify the program. SMART-S remains as a focused tool for BOI-sector technical talent.

Cost

SMART Visa is free at the One Stop Service Center for the visa itself. Expect ฿1,000–฿5,000 in document translation, courier, and notary costs. No agent needed — the OSS center is designed for direct applicant service.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I get a SMART Visa if my employer isn't BOI-promoted? No. SMART-S requires employment in a BOI-promoted target industry. Confirm with the BOI website using your employer's company registration number.
What if I'm self-employed in tech? SMART Startup track was discontinued in 2025. Self-employed tech professionals now look at LTR Highly-Skilled (if you meet income/expertise) or DTV (for remote work).
Is SMART better than Non-B + work permit? For BOI-eligible roles, yes — 3-4 year term, no separate work permit, family work authorization. For non-BOI roles, you can't get SMART; Non-B is the path.
Can I bring my family? Yes. Spouse and children under 20 get SMART dependent visas with the same term as yours. Importantly, dependents can also work in Thailand.
How long does SMART approval take? Typically 30 days from complete application. The BOI endorsement is the long pole — get this lined up before applying.
Does SMART have a tax exemption? No. SMART Visa holders pay regular Thai income tax. For tax exemption, you need LTR Wealthy Pensioner or Wealthy Global Citizen.
What were the discontinued tracks (T, I, E, O)? T was Talent (researchers, scientists). I was Investor (฿20M minimum). E was Executive. O was Other (spouse/dependents). Talent and Investor moved to LTR; Executive merged with S; Other absorbed as standard dependents.
Can I switch from SMART to LTR? Yes. If your role and income qualify for LTR Highly-Skilled (typically the same role at $80k+), you can apply for LTR at year-3 of SMART or earlier. LTR's 10-year term is the natural upgrade.
Where do I apply for SMART-S? SMART Visa One Stop Service Center in Bangkok at the Government Complex Chaeng Wattana. Or any Thai embassy abroad with prior BOI endorsement. The OSS center is purpose-built for SMART applicants.
Is SMART good for my Pattaya-based tech company hiring? Only if your company is BOI-promoted. Most Pattaya tech companies aren't BOI-promoted — they'll sponsor Non-B + work permit instead. If you specifically want to hire in BOI sectors and qualify for promotion, SMART becomes worth pursuing.
