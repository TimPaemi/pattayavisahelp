Major benefits

  • 4 years validity, multiple entry
  • Annual reporting only (vs 90-day for Non-B)
  • No re-entry permits
  • Work without separate work permit in declared role
  • Spouse can work on SMART-O without separate WP
  • Fast-track at OSOS (Bangkok) or EEC Labour Administration Center (Chonburi)

Pattaya / EEC angle

SMART holders working in EEC-promoted industries (Chonburi/Pattaya area: automotive, electronics, food, robotics, logistics) can use the EEC Labour Administration Center in Chonburi instead of Bangkok OSS for visa issuance, extensions, and reporting. Significant convenience for Pattaya-based EEC professionals.

SMART vs LTR — which is right?

Both are BOI-managed, both reduce reporting to annual, both eliminate re-entry permits. Key differences:

  • SMART requires targeted-industry employer/sector. Tighter sector criteria. 4-year validity.
  • LTR doesn't require targeted-industry (broader). 10-year validity. Tax exemption for foreign income (W/P/T) or 17% flat rate (H).

If you fit BOI sectors: SMART can be faster and simpler. If you have $80k+ income: LTR is broader and longer.

Frequently asked questions

Do SMART holders need a work permit?
No traditional work permit book. SMART grants the right to work for the endorsed employer/role.
Can my spouse work in Thailand on SMART-O?
Yes — SMART-O spouses can work without a separate work permit. Major advantage for dual-career couples.
Is my employer in a targeted industry?
15 BOI-targeted sectors include: automotive, smart electronics, robotics, biotech, digital economy, medical hub, aviation/logistics, financial services. Verify with your employer or our consultation.
