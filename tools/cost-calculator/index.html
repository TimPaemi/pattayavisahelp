What's covered, what's not

  • ✓ Initial visa fee at embassy or airport

  • ✕ Family-member visa fees (estimate per-person separately)

The insight most miss
Privilege Gold's per-month figure looks scary, but at year 6 the original ฿900k membership is paid off — your ongoing cost drops dramatically. LTR's tax exemption (under Royal Decree 743) on foreign income remitted to Thailand can save retirees ฿100k+/year for high-income profiles, which the calculator doesn't model.
