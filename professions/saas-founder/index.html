Thailand visa for SaaS founders

Selling to Thai customers? Hiring Thai engineers? Form a Thai limited company, get Non-B work permit. ฿2M paid-up capital, 4 Thai employees per foreign work permit. Many SaaS founders use a holding-company-in-Singapore + operating-company-in-Thailand structure.

Pathway 4 — Privilege Visa (passive base)

If your SaaS runs largely autonomously and you want pay-once-forget visa stability, Privilege at ฿900k-5M is the path. No work permit (not needed for foreign-incorporated business), Fast Track airport, no income proof annually.

Tax engineering for SaaS founders

The cleanest structures we see at Pattaya Visa Help: (a) Estonia/Singapore holding + Thailand resident on LTR-WGC + Royal Decree 743 exemption; (b) US LLC pass-through + Thailand resident on DTV with sub-180-day visits to avoid tax residency; (c) Thai limited company + LTR Highly-Skilled at 17% flat tax for active local operations. Speak to a tax advisor before committing.
