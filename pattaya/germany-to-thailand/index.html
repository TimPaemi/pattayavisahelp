Germany to Thailand: visa options

Best visa pathways for German citizens moving to Pattaya or anywhere in Thailand. Updated for 2026 rules including the visa-exempt cut and tax changes.

Updated 10 May 2026·Pattaya Visa Help·Independent · No commissions
German citizens · 2026 Thailand visa guide 7 min read·Updated April 2026 Quick take for German citizens

Germans often go for the O-A from Berlin or Frankfurt embassy because the mandatory health insurance is straightforward to source.

Visa-exempt entry: what changed in 2026

German passport holders get 30 days visa-exempt on arrival. Cut from 60 to 30 days April 2026.

If you plan to stay longer than 30 days, plan ahead — get a Tourist e-Visa (60 days) or apply for a long-stay visa from your home country before flying.

Land border cap (Nov 2025) Visa-exempt entries by land border are now capped at 2 per calendar year. Air arrivals are unlimited but each is still 30 days max from April 2026.

Best visa pathways for German citizens

Based on what works in practice for German clients in Pattaya:

Retirement Non-O → 50+, ฿800k or ฿65k/month, annual extensions Retirement O-A → 50+ from home country, mandatory health insurance DTV (Destination Thailand Visa) → 5-year, 180 days/entry, ฿10,000 fee Marriage Non-O → Married to Thai, ฿400k or ฿40k/month LTR Long-Term Resident → Specifically for German citizens

Germany expats in Thailand: the deeper context

Germans are the third-largest Western community in Pattaya after Brits and Russians, with an estimated 15,000 long-term residents in greater Pattaya. The community is centered around East Pattaya and Jomtien, with a reputation for orderly visa compliance and strong representation in restaurant and small-business ownership.

German Renten and the income test

German Renten (state pension) is paid by Deutsche Rentenversicherung worldwide. For Thai retirement visa purposes, your monthly Renten payment is documented by a Rentenbescheinigung (pension certificate) issued by DRV. Combined with private pensions or other income, most German retirees easily clear the ฿65,000/month threshold or the ฿800,000 deposit alternative.

Tax: Germany taxes most pensions at source (Quellensteuer) before payment. Under the Germany-Thailand DTA (1967), German state pensions are taxed only in Germany. Private pensions and other income may be taxable in Thailand if remitted, under post-2024 Thai residency rules — though Royal Decree 743 LTR exemption sidesteps this.

O-A is the German retiree default

More Germans use the O-A retirement visa than Brits or Americans, primarily because German health insurance options for the mandatory O-A coverage are well-established and accepted by Thai embassies. DKV, ARAG, ALC, and Allianz Care all issue Thai-embassy-accepted certificates for German residents.

That said, many long-term German residents in Pattaya have transitioned to Non-O Retirement (no insurance requirement) once they're inside Thailand, reverting to private health insurance based on their needs rather than O-A's specific minimum.

German Embassy in Bangkok

Located on Sathorn Road, Bangkok. Provides apostille service for German documents at €60 per document, Lebensbescheinigung (proof of life) for pension purposes, and emergency consular services. Document submission must be done in person; appointments available online.

Driving licence conversion

German EU driving licences convert directly to Thai driving licences with a residence certificate from immigration. No Thai theory test required. Process at any provincial DLT (Department of Land Transport) office; ~1 hour, ฿205 per licence category.

Pattaya German community

The German community is well-organized: German Catholic Church (Sankt Nikolaus) in Pattaya, Deutsch-Thailaendischer Verein, several German restaurants and bakeries (Ein Stein, Kasten's), and a strong Sunday football league (Pattaya Sport Club). The community skews older but has a growing young-family component since 2020.

FAQ

German citizen FAQs

Welche deutsche Krankenversicherung wird für das O-A Visum akzeptiert? DKV, ARAG, ALC und Allianz Care stellen Thailand-akkreditierte Bescheinigungen aus. Die Police muss mindestens ฿40.000 ambulant + ฿400.000 stationär abdecken. Bestätigen Sie mit der Botschaft, die Ihr O-A ausstellt.
Can my Renten be paid directly to a Thai bank? Yes — Deutsche Rentenversicherung pays internationally via SEPA-equivalent transfers. Set up via DRV Online with your Thai bank's IBAN-equivalent (SWIFT code). Conversion to THB at receiving bank's rate.
Is German private pension (Riester, Rürup) treated differently from Renten in Thailand? All foreign pensions remitted to Thailand are potentially taxable post-2024. Whether they actually trigger tax depends on residency days, DTA articles, and any LTR exemption. Consult a Thai tax advisor familiar with German pension structures.
Where do I get a Lebensbescheinigung in Thailand? German Embassy Bangkok or any honorary German consul in Thailand. Pattaya does not have a German consulate currently — travel to Bangkok. Cost: €17 per certificate. Required annually by Deutsche Rentenversicherung to continue pension payments.
Do EU driving licences convert directly to Thai? Yes. EU EEA licences are recognized for direct conversion to Thai licences with a residence certificate from immigration. No Thai theory or practical test required. Bring original licence, passport, residence cert, medical certificate.
Can I import my car from Germany? Technically yes, but the import duties (200%+ of declared value) make it economically irrational. Most German expats sell their car before moving and buy a Thai-market vehicle. New BMW or Mercedes in Thailand: roughly the same price as Germany due to import-on-the-Thai-side duties.
Is German health insurance valid for hospital visits in Thailand? International policies (Allianz Care, BUPA Global) yes. Domestic German GKV (statutory) typically only reimburses emergency overseas treatment up to limited amounts. Most German residents in Thailand transition to a Thailand-resident or international policy.
Can I marry a Thai national in Germany? Yes — German civil marriage is recognized worldwide. Then apostille the certificate, translate to Thai, register at a Thai Amphoe. Read our foreign marriage legalisation guide for the full process.
Are German wills valid for Thai property? A German will may have effect for Thai assets but enforcement requires Thai-court probate, which is slow. Most German expats with Thai property or significant Thai bank holdings make a separate Thai will. Costs: ฿15k–฿50k drafted by a Thai lawyer.
How is the German community organized in Pattaya? Sankt Nikolaus Catholic church (Pattaya), Deutsch-Thailaendischer Verein (German-Thai association), Pattaya Sport Club (Sunday football), various German restaurants and beer halls. Annual Oktoberfest celebrations in Jomtien.
