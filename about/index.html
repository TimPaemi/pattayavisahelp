01 / WHY WE EXIST

Pattaya needed an honest front door.

Pattaya is full of "visa agents." Some are excellent. Many are mediocre. A few are outright dangerous — the August 2025 ED visa crackdown that revoked nearly 10,000 student visas was full of victims who'd paid trusted-looking agents that turned out to be fake-school operators.

Our entire reason for existing

A trustworthy front door — honest answers about which visa fits, and confident referrals to vetted specialists.

We don't sell visas. We connect you with the right specialist, and we earn referral fees only when our clients become satisfied clients of our partners. Our incentive is your good outcome — not your fee.

02 / OUR PRINCIPLES

Four rules we don't break.

i.

Independent, not commissioned

We don't sell any specific visa. We earn referral fees only when our clients choose to engage one of our vetted partners. That alignment is the whole point.

ii.

Vetted, not advertised

Every specialist in our network has been checked end-to-end — DBD registration, physical office, past-client interviews, fee transparency. We refuse to refer unverified operators.

iii.

Pattaya-based, not generic

We know Jomtien Immigration. We know which agents take shortcuts. We track every Thai visa rule change — the November 2025 land-border cap, the February 2025 SMART reform, the April 2026 visa-exempt cut — monthly.

iv.

Honest about money

Transparent pricing from your specialist before you commit. No hidden fees. No commission games. If a cheaper visa fits your situation, we'll tell you that — even when the more expensive one would pay us more.
