An independent Thailand visa resource, built in Pattaya, for the expats who actually live here.

Why we exist

Pattaya is full of "visa agents". Some are excellent. Many are mediocre. A few are outright dangerous — the 2025 ED visa crackdown that revoked nearly 10,000 student visas was full of victims who'd paid trusted-looking agents that turned out to be fake-school operators.

The expat community has needed a single trustworthy front door — somewhere you can go for honest answers about which visa fits your situation, and confident referrals to specialists who've been thoroughly vetted by people who actually understand the Thai system.

That's what Pattaya Visa Help exists to be. We don't sell visas. We connect you with the right specialist, and we earn referral fees only when our clients become satisfied clients of our partners. Our incentive is your good outcome — not your fee.
