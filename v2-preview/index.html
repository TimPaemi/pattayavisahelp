// Quick answers

FAQ.

Are you an agent? Do you take commissions?

No. PVH is independent. The reply is free because it's free. If you want a specialist (lawyer or agent) we'll match you to a vetted Pattaya one — we don't take a cut from either side. You pay them direct.

Which visa is best for digital nomads in Pattaya?

DTV in 95% of cases. 5 years, 180 days per entry, remote-work permitted, no Thai tax if you don't remit. The full breakdown lives in /dtv/.

Do I have to pay Thai tax on foreign income?

Depends. LTR holders (Royal Decree 743) are exempt from remittance tax on foreign income. Everyone else: foreign income remitted within the tax year of earning is taxable above 150K฿. If you stay 180+ days you're a tax resident. Tax guide →

Can I trust the agents you recommend?

We've personally worked with them. We verify they're registered (Thai immigration consultant or licensed Thai lawyer), check Pantip and farang complaints, run quiet test cases. The shortlist changes — anyone who slips, drops.

How does the contact process actually work?

You send a message (form, email, or WhatsApp). We reply inside 24 hrs (usually faster) with (a) the visa name that fits, (b) the doc list, (c) the realistic cost incl. all hidden fees, (d) the timeline, (e) whether you can DIY or need a specialist. Written, in your inbox, no pressure, no upsell.

Why email + WhatsApp, not phone calls?

Because written answers are better. You get them documented, in your timezone, in your language. You can re-read them. We can include links to the right guide on our site. Phone calls would mean overlapping timezones, language friction, and you'd have to retain the info. Email + WhatsApp wins for everyone.
